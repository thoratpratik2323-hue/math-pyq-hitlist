CBSE 10-Year PYQ Hitlist 🎯

CBSE Class 12th Math ke woh strictly selected topics jo har saal board paper mein aate hi hain! Prepare these to guarantee 80/80 marks.

⚠️

Pratik's Target Rule for Ishu:

Sirf book padhne se topper nahi bante! In topics se Har Chapter ke atleast 5 previous year questions (2014-2023) paper pe solve karke practice karna zaruri hai. CBSE apna pattern inhi topics ke ird-gird set karta hai!

01
Relations & Functions ~ 4 Marks
  • Equivalence Relation Proof: Questions showing relation R is reflexive, symmetric & transitive (usually set of integers / modulus relations).
  • Bijective Proofs: Prove function f(x) is One-One and Onto.
02
Inverse Trigo ~ 4 Marks
  • Principal Values: Finding values of sin⁻¹(sin(2π/3)) or tan⁻¹(-√3) keeping domains in mind.
  • Simplest Form: Substituting x=tanθ / sinθ to simplify complex ITFs.
03
Matrices ~ 5 Marks
  • Symmetric & Skew: Express matrix as a sum of symmetric & skew-symmetric matrices.
  • Multiplication properties: Prove A² - 5A + 7I = 0 type equality equations.
04
Determinants ~ 5 Marks
  • Matrix Method (Long Q): Solving system of 3 linear equations using X = A⁻¹B. (Guaranteed 5-Marker).
  • Adjoint properties: 1-mark MCQs on |adj A| = |A|ⁿ⁻¹.
05
Continuity & Diff. ~ 8 Marks
  • Find value of k: If function is continuous at x=a, find unknown 'k' or 'a,b'.
  • Logarithmic Diff: Derivative of (sinx)ˣ + x^(sinx) using log.
  • Double Derivative: Prove that d²y/dx² - y = 0 type questions.
06
Application of Derivatives ~ 9 Marks
  • Maxima/Minima Word Problems: Maximize volume of cylinder in cone/sphere or maximize area of printing page. (Very Important Case Study / Long Question).
  • Increasing/Decreasing: Find intervals where function is strictly increasing.
07
Integrals ~ 9 Marks
  • King's Property: Definite integrals using ∫f(x) = ∫f(a-x) property, specially evaluating ∫(√x)/(√x + √(a-x)) or Modulus function integrals.
  • Partial Fractions: Integrals type 1/((x+1)(x+2)).
  • By Parts: Special rule integral of eˣ[f(x) + f'(x)].
08
Application of Integrals ~ 6 Marks
  • Area of Bounded Region: Area between parabola and line (y=x², y=x) or circle and line.
  • Sketching curves: Accurately drawing standard curves and finding enclosed area.
09
Differential Equations ~ 7 Marks
  • Linear DE (LDE): Form dy/dx + Py = Q, finding Integrating Factor (I.F) and solving.
  • Homogeneous DE: Substituting y=vx and solving for variables separable.
10
Vector Algebra ~ 7 Marks
  • Dot/Cross Product: Finding area of triangle/parallelogram or projecting vector A on B.
  • Perpendicular Vectors: Use condition A.B = 0 to find unknown scalars.
11
3D Geometry ~ 7 Marks
  • Shortest Distance: Finding shortest distance between two skew lines. (Another 100% predictable Question).
  • Image of Point: Finding foot of perpendicular and image of a point in a line.
12
Linear Programming ~ 5 Marks
  • Corner Point Graphical Method: Maximize or Minimize Z=ax+by subject to 3-4 constraints. Identifying feasible region properly.
13
Probability ~ 8 Marks
  • Bayes' Theorem: The ultimate question asked every year (e.g., probability of choosing a specific bag, disease testing).
  • Probability Distribution: Finding Mean/Expected value E(X) from random experiment chart.
  • Conditional Prob: Direct formula based questions P(A|B).
